    YOU ARE HIGHLY WELCOME!!!! TO SNAP SCHOOL

    On October 3rd, 1977, SNAP School officially welcomed 5 students to the school. With PROFESSOR YINKUS, the head teacher, Mrs OYENSOR, assistant teacher, and Mrs Loda, the admin officer, she and her team had a goal to provide the nurturing environment to give the students the best private education in Nigeria. By the end of the academic year, the students had grown to 31. A strong Christian by faith, she thought of the name All Saints School, but the name was deemed too "religious", and instead, decided on SNAP School.
